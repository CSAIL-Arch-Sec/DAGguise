== Alice in Cyberwonderland - A Teaching Tale ==

:'''4. ... Wikibooks primary mission is to create open source textbooks, how-to guides, and classroom aids.'' - from [[Wikibooks:What Wikibooks is not]]

:'''Alice in Cyberwonderland''' - and any such sequels as might emerge, e.g. '''Alice Through the Windows''', '''Alice's Adventures in Wikiwonderland''', although on the surface a work of fiction and fantasy, is being written as an imaginative vehicle of discovery of the wonders of a digital universe: a universe not subject to the constraints of the laws of conservation of mass and energy - as everything that lives in a purely digital form has '''zero mass''', '''zero physical size''', with '''virtually zero travel time''', a universe governed by the laws and properties of information, information systems; a world where the nture of proerty has become transformed, with the nature of information itself favouring the freedom of expression, exploration. inquiry and exchnage that a wiki, open source, open content, interactive information powered by evolving interoperable digital engines whose creative work consumes virtually no energy, where the same entity can be in many places at the same time and in many different forms ...

:Among the specific examples of how Alice's adventures open up exploration and discovering of the naure of digital universes are the '''light and colour cubes'''[http://www.light-cubes.net] - soon to be the basis for another Wikibook - and her discovery of the state of the '''[[Mesopotamian Marshlands]]''' will led her into a journey through time and space where becomes a participant in the emergence of a new knowledge-based civilization in which the restoration of the decimated Mesopotamian Marshes in Southern Iraq serves as a catalyst for a rebirth in the cradle of civilization.

:Alice also becomes immersed in a world of '''information species''' - a few of which have already been identified in the current draft - and begins to become aware of the wonders of information ecosystems and [[Information Ecology]].

=== A tribute to Lewis Carroll ===

:A self-revealing light-hearted tribute to '''Lewis Carroll''' - ''aka'' '''Rev. Charles Dodson'' - and a choice to follow Rev. Dodson's footsteps of anonymity.

:An exploration of a different wonderland, one that did not exist in Lewis Carroll's time. If Lewis Carroll were alive now, might not he - as a mathematician - have been fascinated by the wonders of digital technology. Might he not have conjured up tales for a modern-day Alice that took place in today's '''lookinglass''' - plus microscope and telescope - world of the Internet?

=== A tribute to mathematics ===

:The commmon bond of mathematics is a special bridge to the mind to Lewis Carroll, for the '''mathematics of information''' - including zero mass, zero physical size, and virtually zero travel time - and fields such as '''chaos mathematics''', '''mathematical biology''' and more have been among the numerous sources of inspiration to John Doe's alter ego in his explorations and appreciation of the dynamics of a digital knowledge-based universe.

=== An imaginary journey through the wonders of a digital universe ===

:For the tale that reveals itself, is a tale of global transofrmation - transition to a center of gravity - and levity too - a new '''Nash Equilibirum''' based on the properties and dynamics of the digital knowledge-based universe that many '''John Doe'''s - and '''Jane Doe'''s too - discover themselves immersed in the vast, wonderful and mysterious information ecosystem - see, for example, the wikibook on '''Information Ecology'''[http://en.wikibooks.org/wiki/Information_Ecology] - with virtually no boundaries, and virtually free from the constraints of the laws of conservation of mass and energy.

=== Improvisatonal Plays ===

:In a world that at time can be osessed with '''work''', Alice in Cyberwonderland is a space for '''improvisational play''' - playing with words and meanings, with numbers and mathematical expresions, plays of apparent illusion and disillusion - plays of paradox ... and in the process, rediscovering how we can - and do - learn through interactive play and games.

=== An Invitation to explore and discover Cyberwonderland ===

== Revelation of a "Gaia Mind"? ==

:Perhaps it will become '''self-evident''' that what is arising through the birth and evolution of new forms of collective intelligence is no less than the manifestation of a '''Gaia Mind''' - a mind that serves as the key to a new era in the life of Gaia - the Earth and the commuity of life of the Earth

=== Catalyst: the Mesopotamian Marshlands ===

:Who knows? Perhaps the regeneration of the '''Mesopotamian Marshlands'''[http://www.mesopotamian-marshlands.net] - ''between the rivers - a cradle of civilization'' - may be the venue through which clear evidence of the presence and workings of a Gaia Mind become revealed - '''hotspots''', '''tension points''' that reflect the convergence of almost all of the dimensions of tension and conflict on the Earth and that may, going beyond the '''canary in the coal mine''', may not only be alerting us to the perils of the distortion of '''moral and economic values''' associated with a world addicted to '''unsustainable fossil-fuel-dependence''' and a reliance on military force and '''weapons of destruction''' to suppress conflict, rather than a way that '''honours the earth''', treasures the '''healing powers of hwater''', and where civilization and the laws of the land uphold nonviolent, '''peaceful resolution of conflict'''.
